BNY Mellon Wealth Management

  • This was a 9 month long project (3 months of research, concept, and detail design.) I designed a site for the wealth management services of BNY Mellon - America's longest standing financial institution. I came up with a new design language for Wealth Management. I designed 16 templates for desktop and mobile. I also created an extensive style guide to document the design system.
